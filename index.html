Griot Chinyere

Storyteller / Educator / Writer

Performance Enhanced: Personified...

International storyteller, educator, community entrepreneur, theatre director founder of Storytelling charity Shanti-Chi and the Nne Agwu African Storytelling Festival.

Griot Chinyere blends her skills as a movement stylist, percussionist, mimic and performer to share dynamic and lyrical stories from around the world.

She combines Rhythm and drama into a myriad of diverse folktales, legends and stories for all as an exploration of the correlation between the art and structure of storytelling and spirituality.

She aspires to serve the youth in the Caribbean and African continent, connecting with communities, producing workshops that educate, enlighten and entertain.
